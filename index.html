LCF Funla nguages are the perfect low-cost opportunity for those looking to make a living and build a business around teaching languages.

What can I earn?

This depends on the schools, venues and areas you decide to work in. To give you an idea, our club system allow you to earn E50+ per hour, but larger clubs can earn you more. Working just 6-8 hours per week at lunch- times or after school, you could match many full time jobs for income, with plenty of time to spare for family, or to grow and expand your business. You can do this by hiring other teachers to work for you.

Do I have to make my own resources?

No, the great thing about joining LCF is the access to all our detailed lesson plans which cover every contact session. You can teach beginners clubs, nursery clubs, advanced clubs and even online clubs with our materials. If you would like to teach in schools we also have a full in school course for French and Spanish and all of our courses come with activities and resources, and membership packs or exercise books. Not to mention our SMART-accredited interactive website, Babelzone. There is no need to research schemes or find the best resources because you will be provided with everything you need.
